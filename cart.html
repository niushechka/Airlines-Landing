Alaska Airlines

Alaska Airlines is the main airline of the United States of America with headquarters in SeaTac, a suburb of Seattle (Washington State), USA.

The airline uses Seattle/Tacoma International Airport and Theda Stevens Anchorage International Airport as its main transit hubs; additional hubs are located at Portland International Airport and Los Angeles International Airport.
